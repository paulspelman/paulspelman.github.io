Our Work Is Not Done

In 2012 President Obama ran for a second term. He'd achieved a lot during his first term, but there was more progress to be made.

"Our Work Is Not Done" lists 500 of Obama's accomplishments and goals. Each item corresponds to a point in a dot-to-dot image. As items are crossed off, the image of the president takes shape.
