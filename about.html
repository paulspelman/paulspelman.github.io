About Me
Hello there!

I’m an experienced and accomplished creative director and writer with 12 years of experience, currently freelancing in NYC. I’ve worked in New Zealand, Australia and the U.S. on a wide variety of clients and causes.

I’m passionate about bringing great ideas to life and I truly believe that outstanding creative work leads to outstanding business results.

My background is in copywriting, but I love learning new things and expanding my skill set. I’ve also worked as an art director, I’ve dabbled in design, I can edit video, and recently I’ve learned how to build websites from scratch.

This broad experience means I can collaborate with, and successfully lead, all types of creative teams.

At the moment I’m interested in new ways to tell stories online, entreprenurial agency models, and world-class craft across all disciplines and mediums.

Get in touch if you’d like to collaborate on an interesting project, or chat about OKC basketball.
