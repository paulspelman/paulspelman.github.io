Head Scratchers

When you scratch your head at the wrong time, you look confused.

We brought this insight to life with a radio and print campaign for Head & Shoulders Itchy Scalp Care Shampoo. The print executions were made by hand with cut paper.

The "CEO" and "Date" radio executions were shortlisted at Cannes.
