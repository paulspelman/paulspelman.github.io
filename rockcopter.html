Converse is all about celebrating and encouraging the creative spirit of their young fans.

For their Get Loud competition, they wanted these fans to film themselves "getting loud" in a public place - being provocative, rowdy, and creative.

We created this video to inspire the fans and kick-start entries. It's a speaker playing very loud death metal strapped to a remote control helicopter. Good times!
